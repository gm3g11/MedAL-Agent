## Throughput fix delivered (the main Stage-1 win)
The fg-stratify pre-scan loaded **every** train mask (21,507 NIfTI slices for msd07) and
re-ran per cell. Now **cached per (dataset, seed)** â†’ scan runs once. Validated: btcv scan
40 s (miss) â†’ 0.013 s (hit). Plus the preprocessed-array cache and resolution-keyed SAM
cache (`__in512`). For Stage 1, `precompute_sam` warms SAM up front so P7/P8 are cache hits.

## Determinism bug found + fixed (important for Stage 1)
Determinism held at 128 but **broke at 512**: `cudnn.deterministic=True` alone doesn't
cover all CUDA ops at larger resolutions (conv-transpose backward uses atomics), so
same-seed runs produced different round-0 weights. Fixed in `runner/seeds.py` by adding
`torch.use_deterministic_algorithms(True, warn_only=True)` + `CUBLAS_WORKSPACE_CONFIG=:4096:8`.
Verified: two identical busi/P1 @512 runs â†’ identical selected_ids + ckpt_hash. 137 tests pass.
